About SongSlang

What is SongSlang?

SongSlang is an app that allows you to look up the lyrics of any of your favorite songs, and then search both the Urban Dictionary and Merriam Webster definitions of any words in those songs. Simply type in the name of the song and the artist, and the lyrics as well as search functions for both dictionaries will appear. Alternatively, you can head over to the definitions page and look up definitions in both dictionaries without providing song lyrics. We hope you enjoy the app! SongSlang uses the following databases to access information:



Lyrics.ovh is the database used to access the lyrics.

LyricsOvh

Urban Dictionary is used to access slang term definitions.

Urban Dictionary

Merriam Webster is used to access dictionary definitions.

Merriam Webster

TheAudioDB is used to access artist images.

TheAudioDB

About The Team

SongSlang was developed by Team Awesome aka Sle Ahn, Alex Noriega, and Michael Scharf for their first project at UC Irvine coding bootcamp. The idea came when they realized that many words in songs are either not pronounced clearly or are not commonly used.
